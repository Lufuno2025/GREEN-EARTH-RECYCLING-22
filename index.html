Frequently Asked Questions

Visit our Contact page and complete the Request Pickup form with address, preferred day and type of materials. We'll respond with available slots and confirm the booking.

We accept paper, cardboard, certain plastics (clean and sorted), glass, metal, and electronic waste via scheduled bulky-item collections. Check Services for details.

Residential pickups have standard rates depending on frequency and volume; some community events and educational sessions are free. Request a quote on the Contact page.

Use the Enquiry or Contact page to express interest — choose 'Volunteer' or 'Sponsorship' in the enquiry form. Our community coordinator will get back to you.
