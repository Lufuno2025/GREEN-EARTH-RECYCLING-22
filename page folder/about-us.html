About GreenEarth Recycling

GreenEarth Recycling was established in 2017 in Cape Town to address local waste challenges by combining collection services, community education and partnerships with local businesses and schools.

Mission

To reduce waste and inspire communities to reuse and recycle through practical, accessible programs.
